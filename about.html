About Canyon Tutors

Founded in 2025, Canyon Tutors empowers middle and high school students with effective and affordable tutoring and test prep.

Based in Provo, Utah, we offer in-person tutoring throughout Salt Lake and Utah counties. For students anywhere else, we provide the same personalized experience through virtual sessions.

Our tutors are top-performing students and graduates who know what it takes to succeed academically. We specialize in math, science, English, history, and standardized test prep including the ACT and SAT.

Whether you need weekly homework help or intensive test preparation, Canyon Tutors provides a supportive, one-on-one learning environment tailored to each student's goals.
