Frequently Asked Questions

What is the class size?

Classes have 15 or fewer students for personalized attention.

Do I get personalized feedback?

Yes, the classes are interactive and each student receives four 30-minute 1-on-1 online sessions. You'll never have a question go unanswered.

Are classes online or in-person?

Classes are in-person in Utah, with Zoom for 1-on-1 sessions.

What if I can’t attend in person?

Classes are streamed and recorded for flexible access.

What materials are included?

We provide practice tests, worksheets, and guides.

Why bother with the class?

At Canyon Tutors, we want to keep tutoring effective and affordable. This hybrid class allows you to get personalized instruction without paying an arm and a leg.

Is there homework?

The most essential part of ACT prep is practice. But don't worry: we've got you covered. We know high school is busy, so only expect to practice 30 minutes outside of class each week, and we'll review it one-on-one so you can get everything done.
