Course Structure

The course runs over 4 weeks, with structured sessions to maximize retention and improvement. Each week will start with a 2-hour class instruction and will include a 30-minute personal virtual session.

Week 1

Introduction & strategies for English and Math sections. Includes practice problems and guided review.

Week 2

Focus on Reading strategies and time management. 1-on-1 Zoom check-in to identify personal strengths/weaknesses.

Week 3

Science section strategies and integrating practice tests. Group discussions to reinforce concepts.

Week 4

Full practice test review, scoring strategies, and final prep tips. Final 1-on-1 session for personalized guidance.
